## 🇺🇸 United States — H-1B Visa

### Overview
| Field | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **Visa Type** | Employer-sponsored, non-immigrant specialty occupation visa |
| **Duration** | 3 years (renewable up to 6 years; extendable with I-140 approval) |
| **Annual Quota** | 85,000/year (65,000 regular + 20,000 US Master's holders) |
| **Average Salary** | USD $110,000 – $180,000/year |

### ✅ Eligibility Requirements
- Bachelor's or higher degree in a specialty occupation (CS, IT, Engineering)
- Job offer from a US employer willing to sponsor
- Employer must file Labor Condition Application (LCA) with DOL
- Pass the H-1B lottery (registration opens every March)
- No criminal record; pass medical exam

### 📋 Application Process
1. **March**: Employer registers you in H-1B lottery ($215 fee)
2. If selected, employer files Form I-129 with USCIS ($460–$730)
3. USCIS processes petition (3–6 months; 15 days premium for $2,805)
4. Apply for H-1B visa stamp at US Embassy/Consulate in India
5. Attend visa interview with DS-160, I-797 approval, passport, photos
6. Enter USA on or after **October 1st** (start of fiscal year)

### 🏠 PR Pathway
Apply for Green Card (EB-2/EB-3) via employer sponsorship. Wait time for Indians: **10–50+ years** due to per-country backlog.

### 💡 Pro Tips
> Apply through companies with strong H-1B sponsorship history (TCS, Infosys, Google, Microsoft). Consider the **O-1 visa** as an alternative if you have exceptional skills/achievements.

## 🇨🇦 Canada — Express Entry (Federal Skilled Worker)

### Overview
| Field | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **Visa Type** | Points-based permanent residence (not a temporary visa) |
| **Duration** | Permanent Residence (renewable every 5 years) |
| **Annual Quota** | No fixed cap; draws every 2 weeks based on CRS score |
| **Average Salary** | CAD $90,000 – $140,000/year |

### ✅ Eligibility Requirements
- CRS score typically 470–520+ (varies per draw)
- At least 1 year of skilled work experience (NOC TEER 0/1/2/3)
- IELTS/CELPIP: CLB 7+ in all 4 bands
- WES Educational Credential Assessment (ECA) for Indian degree
- Valid job offer or provincial nomination boosts CRS significantly

### 📋 Application Process
1. Get IELTS or CELPIP score (CLB 7+)
2. Get WES ECA for your Indian degree
3. Create Express Entry profile at IRCC website
4. Enter pool and wait for Invitation to Apply (ITA)
5. Submit complete PR application within 60 days of ITA
6. Biometrics, medical exam, police clearance certificate (PCC)
7. Receive Confirmation of Permanent Residence (COPR)

### 🏠 PR Pathway
This IS the PR pathway. Eligible for **citizenship after 3 years** of physical presence as PR.

### 💡 Pro Tips
> Provincial Nominee Programs (PNP) like Ontario Tech Draw or BC PNP Tech add **600 CRS points**, virtually guaranteeing an ITA. Explore these first before the federal pool.

## 🇬🇧 United Kingdom — Skilled Worker Visa

### Overview
| Field | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **Visa Type** | Employer-sponsored work visa |
| **Duration** | Up to 5 years (extendable; leads to ILR after 5 years) |
| **Annual Quota** | No cap for most tech roles |
| **Average Salary** | GBP £35,000 – £80,000/year |

### ✅ Eligibility Requirements
- Job offer from a UK Home Office-licensed sponsor employer
- Role on the eligible occupation list (most software roles qualify)
- Salary meets minimum threshold: **£38,700** or role's going rate
- English: B1 level (IELTS 4.0+ or degree taught in English)
- Maintenance funds: £1,270 in bank for 28 days (unless employer certifies)

### 📋 Application Process
1. Secure job offer from a licensed UK sponsor
2. Employer issues Certificate of Sponsorship (CoS)
3. Apply online (£827 up to 3 yrs / £1,636 for 3–5 yrs)
4. Pay Immigration Health Surcharge (£1,035/year)
5. Biometrics at a Visa Application Centre in India
6. Receive decision (typically 3–8 weeks)

### 🏠 PR Pathway
Apply for **Indefinite Leave to Remain (ILR)** after 5 years. Citizenship after 1 more year.

### 💡 Pro Tips
> Verify your employer is on the Home Office register of licensed sponsors before accepting an offer. The **Global Talent visa** is a strong alternative for senior engineers.

## 🇦🇺 Australia — Subclass 482 TSS / 189 / 190

### Overview
| Field | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **Visa Type** | Employer-sponsored (482) or points-based permanent (189/190) |
| **Duration** | 482: 2–4 years; 189/190: Permanent Residence |
| **Annual Quota** | No fixed cap |
| **Average Salary** | AUD $100,000 – $160,000/year |

### ✅ Eligibility Requirements
- ACS (Australian Computer Society) skills assessment for ICT roles
- Points test score of 65+ (age, English, experience, education)
- IELTS 6.0+ (competent English); 7.0+ preferred
- Occupation on MLTSSL/STSOL skilled occupation list
- For 482: employer sponsorship and job offer required

### 📋 Application Process
1. Get ACS skills assessment (submit qualifications + experience docs)
2. Achieve required IELTS/PTE score
3. Submit Expression of Interest (EOI) in SkillSelect
4. Wait for state or federal invitation based on points
5. Lodge visa application within 60 days of invitation
6. Health examination and police clearance
7. Biometrics and visa grant

### 🏠 PR Pathway
189/190 are direct PR pathways. 482 holders can apply for **subclass 186** (TRT stream) after 3 years with the same employer.

### 💡 Pro Tips
> State sponsorship (190) adds **5 extra points** and improves chances significantly. Victoria, NSW, and Queensland have active tech-focused draws.

## 🇩🇪 Germany — EU Blue Card / Skilled Immigration Act

### Overview
| Field | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **Visa Type** | Employment-based work visa (leads to PR) |
| **Duration** | Blue Card: 4 years (or contract duration + 3 months) |
| **Annual Quota** | No cap |
| **Average Salary** | EUR €55,000 – €90,000/year |

### ✅ Eligibility Requirements
- Recognized university degree (check anabin.kmk.org for Indian degrees)
- Job offer meeting salary threshold: **€45,300** general / **€41,041** for IT (shortage occupation)
- German language not mandatory for Blue Card but helps for PR
- Health insurance coverage

### 📋 Application Process
1. Find job and get offer from German employer
2. Check degree recognition at anabin.kmk.org
3. Apply for National Visa (D-type) at German Embassy/Consulate in India
4. Submit: passport, job offer, degree certs, salary confirmation, health insurance
5. Visa issued in 4–12 weeks
6. Arrive → apply for EU Blue Card at local Ausländerbehörde
7. Register at local Bürgeramt within 14 days of arrival

### 🏠 PR Pathway
**Niederlassungserlaubnis** (settlement permit) after 21 months with Blue Card + B1 German, or 33 months with A1 German. Citizenship after 5–8 years.

### 💡 Pro Tips
> Germany's **Opportunity Card (Chancenkarte)** allows you to enter for 1 year to search for a job — great if you don't have an offer yet. IT is a shortage occupation, so the salary threshold is lower.

## 🇳🇱 Netherlands — Highly Skilled Migrant (Kennismigrant)

### Overview
| Field | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **Visa Type** | Employer-sponsored work permit |
| **Duration** | Up to 5 years (tied to employment) |
| **Annual Quota** | No cap |
| **Average Salary** | EUR €65,000 – €100,000/year (Amsterdam) |

### ✅ Eligibility Requirements
- Job offer from an IND-recognized sponsor employer
- Salary above threshold: **€46,107+/year** (under 30: €33,716+)
- No formal degree requirement (salary-based system)
- Valid passport
- 30% Tax Ruling available (significant tax benefit)

### 📋 Application Process
1. Secure offer from an IND-recognized Dutch employer
2. Employer applies to IND on your behalf (~2–4 weeks)
3. Receive MVV (Provisional Residence Permit) authorization
4. Apply for entry visa at Dutch Embassy in India
5. Register with municipality (BRP) within 5 days of arrival
6. Collect residence permit (verblijfsvergunning) from IND desk

### 🏠 PR Pathway
Permanent residence after 5 years. EU long-term residence permit available. Citizenship after 5 years.

### 💡 Pro Tips
> The **30% ruling** allows 30% of your salary to be paid tax-free for 5 years. Major tech employers (Booking.com, ASML, Adyen, TomTom) are headquartered here.

## 🇸🇬 Singapore — Employment Pass (EP)

### Overview
| Field | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **Visa Type** | Employer-sponsored professional work pass |
| **Duration** | 1–2 years initially (renewable) |
| **Annual Quota** | No quota (subject to FCF scrutiny) |
| **Average Salary** | SGD $5,000 – $15,000+/month |

### ✅ Eligibility Requirements
- Job offer from Singapore-registered employer
- Acceptable degree from recognized university
- Monthly salary of **SGD $5,000+** (higher for older candidates)
- Employer must advertise on MyCareersFuture for 28 days (FCF)
- COMPASS points framework: must score 40+ points

### 📋 Application Process
1. Employer submits EP application via EP Online (MOM portal)
2. Processing: 3–8 weeks
3. Receive In-Principle Approval (IPA) letter
4. Enter Singapore within 6 months of IPA date
5. Register with MOM and receive EP card
6. Open bank account, register SingPass

### 🏠 PR Pathway
Apply for **Permanent Residence** after 2 years on EP. Citizenship after 2 years as PR. PR is competitive — strong employment history and community ties help.

### 💡 Pro Tips
> Singapore has **zero capital gains tax** and low personal income tax. The Tech Bonus under COMPASS awards extra points for shortage tech skills (AI, cybersecurity, data).

## 🇳🇿 New Zealand — Accredited Employer Work Visa (AEWV)

### Overview
| Field | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **Visa Type** | Employer-sponsored work visa |
| **Duration** | Up to 3 years (extendable) |
| **Annual Quota** | No cap |
| **Average Salary** | NZD $60,000 – $110,000/year |

### ✅ Eligibility Requirements
- Job offer from an INZ-accredited NZ employer
- Role pays at or above median wage: **NZD $31.61/hour**
- Relevant qualifications or work experience
- Health and character requirements (medical exam, PCC)

### 📋 Application Process
1. Employer achieves INZ accreditation
2. Employer submits Job Check (confirms role can't be filled locally)
3. Apply for AEWV online at Immigration New Zealand
4. Submit passport, job offer, qualifications, character/health docs
5. Processing: 4–8 weeks
6. Arrive and register with IRD for tax

### 🏠 PR Pathway
Apply for **Skilled Migrant Category Residence** after 2 years on AEWV (if earning 1.5x median wage). Citizenship after 5 years.

### 💡 Pro Tips
> New Zealand's tech sector is growing fast in Auckland and Wellington. Quality of life and a welcoming Indian community make it a great lifestyle destination.

## 🇮🇪 Ireland — Critical Skills Employment Permit (CSEP)

### Overview
| Field | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **Visa Type** | Employment permit for in-demand occupations |
| **Duration** | 2 years (renewable; leads to PR) |
| **Annual Quota** | No cap for Critical Skills occupations |
| **Average Salary** | EUR €38,000 – €90,000+/year |

### ✅ Eligibility Requirements
- Job offer in a Critical Skills occupation (software dev roles qualify)
- Relevant degree or higher qualification
- Salary of **€38,000+** (most software roles); €64,000+ for top-tier
- Employer registered in Ireland and tax compliant

### 📋 Application Process
1. Secure job offer from Irish employer
2. Apply online at Employment Permits Online System (EPOS)
3. Processing: 4–6 weeks
4. Apply for Irish Entry Visa (D type) at Irish Embassy in India
5. Enter Ireland and register with GNIB/IRP
6. Collect Irish Residence Permit (IRP card)

### 🏠 PR Pathway
**Stamp 4** (work without permit) after 2 years. Long-term residence after 5 years. Citizenship after 5 years.

### 💡 Pro Tips
> Ireland hosts European HQs of Google, Meta, Amazon, and Microsoft. Corporate tax of 12.5% makes it a magnet for big tech. Dublin has a large, established Indian tech community.

## 🇦🇪 UAE — Employment Visa / Golden Visa

### Overview
| Field | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **Visa Type** | Employer-sponsored or self-sponsored long-term visa |
| **Duration** | Employment Visa: 2 years; Golden Visa: 10 years |
| **Annual Quota** | No cap |
| **Average Salary** | AED 15,000 – 50,000/month (tax-free) |

### ✅ Eligibility Requirements
- For Employment Visa: job offer from UAE employer
- For Golden Visa: salary AED 30,000+/month OR specialized tech/science talent
- Degree attestation: HRD Ministry India → MEA → UAE Embassy
- Medical fitness test in UAE
- Emirates ID registration

### 📋 Application Process
1. Get job offer; employer initiates visa process
2. Attest degrees: State HRD → MEA → UAE Embassy in India
3. Enter UAE on visit/employment visa
4. Medical fitness test at UAE authorized center
5. Emirates ID biometrics at GDRFA/ICA
6. Receive residence visa stamped in passport
7. For Golden Visa: apply via ICA Smart Services portal

### 🏠 PR Pathway
UAE does not offer traditional PR/citizenship to most expats. Golden Visa provides **10-year renewable residency**. A 2021 law allows naturalization for exceptional talent.

### 💡 Pro Tips
> Zero income tax makes UAE very attractive financially. Dubai and Abu Dhabi have growing tech hubs. Many Indian professionals use UAE as a long-term base. Factor in the high cost of living.

## 📁 General Tips for Indian Applicants

### 📄 Documents to Keep Ready
- [ ] Passport (valid 6+ months beyond intended stay)
- [ ] All degree certificates (B.Tech/MCA/M.Tech) — originals + copies
- [ ] HRD Ministry attestation for degrees
- [ ] MEA apostille where required
- [ ] Experience letters from all employers
- [ ] Salary slips (last 3–6 months)
- [ ] Bank statements (last 6 months)
- [ ] PAN card, Aadhaar card
- [ ] IELTS/TOEFL scores
- [ ] Police Clearance Certificate (PCC)

### 🌐 Top Job Platforms
- **LinkedIn Jobs** — Best for global tech roles
- **Glassdoor** — Salary insights + listings
- **Indeed** (country-specific) — indeed.co.uk, indeed.com.au
- **Naukri Gulf** — UAE and Middle East
- **Job Bank Canada** — jobbank.gc.ca
- **EURES** — European Union jobs
- **Wellfound** — Global startup opportunities

### 💰 Financial Planning
- Compare post-tax salaries — UAE is tax-free; Germany/Netherlands have ~35–45% tax
- Check if 30% Tax Ruling applies (Netherlands) or similar expat tax benefits
- Open a **Wise** or **Revolut** account for international money transfers
- Keep an **NRE account** in India for tax-free repatriation of foreign income

### ⚠️ Common Mistakes to Avoid
- Applying to employers who aren't approved/licensed sponsors
- Not getting degree attestation done in advance (takes 4–8 weeks)
- Missing PCC validity — most countries require PCC issued within 6 months
- Underestimating H-1B lottery odds — always have a backup country plan
- Not consulting a licensed immigration lawyer for complex cases
